What is it that you hope to explore during your residency?

We are exploring the protocols, languages, and material of the internet as a place for experimentation and practice. The exploration is based on composing multiple histories of the internet into a collective resyndicatable digital garden to weave the examples, narratives, and ideas together as a contribution to the Field Guide to the Digital Real. We are interested in how this garden can serve as a site for [micro] world-making as we repurpose the existing internet through our practice.
